Tab Out Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Data Collected

Tab Out collects tab URLs, tab titles, and user preferences (theme, settings) solely to provide its functionality.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local and chrome.storage.sync. No data is ever transmitted to any external server.

Data Sharing

None. Tab Out uses no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services. Your data stays on your device.

User Control

You can delete all stored data at any time through the extension settings, or by removing the extension from Chrome.

Permissions

  • tabs — Required to read open tab URLs and titles for grouping and display.
  • storage — Required to save your preferences and saved-for-later tabs locally.
